The Promotion

BUILT TO
SHOWCASE
THE SPORT.

Showcase Muay Thai is a new fight promotion dedicated to putting on real, competitive, sanctioned Muay Thai events across Connecticut. We're here to give fighters a stage and fans an experience.

This isn't backyard fighting — it's authentic Muay Thai with licensed officials, proper matchmaking, medical staff on site, and a fight night atmosphere that respects both the sport and the athletes.

We welcome fighters from all gyms across New England. If your athlete is ready to compete, we want to hear from you.

How It Works

FANS &
FIGHTERS

Two ways to be part of Showcase Muay Thai. Whether you're coming to watch or stepping into the ring, here's everything you need to know.

For Fans

COME
WATCH.

Showcase events are open to the public. We put on a real fight night — multiple bouts, live commentary, a charged atmosphere, and authentic Muay Thai competition from novice to pro.

1

Sign up for notifications. We'll email you the moment the event date and venue are confirmed.

2

Tickets go live. Grab yours early — general admission and reserved seating will be available.

3

Show up and watch. Doors open one hour before the first bout. Full card posted in advance.

For Fighters

STEP UP
AND FIGHT.

We're actively building our inaugural fight card. Novice, amateur, and professional fighters are all welcome. We work directly with coaches and gyms to build fair, competitive matchups.

1

Submit an inquiry. Tell us your fighter's weight class, experience level, and availability. All gyms welcome.

2

Matchmaking. We find a competitive, appropriate opponent. Fighter safety and fairness come first.

3

Confirmation & fight night. Get your contract, weigh-in details, and compete on a real promoted card.

What We Stand For

WHY
SHOWCASE

🥊
Authentic Muay Thai

We run traditional-rules Muay Thai — elbows, knees, clinch and all. This isn't kickboxing. The full art is on display.

⚖️
Fair Matchmaking

Every bout is carefully matched by experience, record, and weight. Competitive fights that push both athletes.

🏥
Safety First

Licensed officials, medical staff on site, and sanctioned oversight. Fighter safety is non-negotiable.

🌎
Open to All Gyms

We don't play favorites. Fighters from any gym across New England are welcome to compete on a Showcase card.

📣
Real Promotion

Live commentary, event photography, social media coverage, and a fight-night atmosphere that treats athletes like pros.

📈
Grow the Sport

Connecticut deserves a dedicated Muay Thai promotion. We're here to build it from the ground up — event by event.
